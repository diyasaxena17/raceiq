# RaceIQ Model Card

Model details will be added after the first pit recommendation and win-likelihood models are trained.

## Planned Models

- Pit recommendation model: recommends whether to pit, stay out, or monitor based on current race state.
- Win-likelihood model: predicts driver and team win probabilities over the next two races.

## Planned Win-Likelihood Inputs

- Track characteristics
- Weather forecasts and historical weather context
- Car and team performance signals
- Driver form and driver-track history
- Team reliability and strategy execution
- Team and media sentiment

## Limitations To Document

- Forecasts are probabilistic, not guarantees.
- Sentiment data can be noisy, biased, stale, or unavailable.
- Car performance proxies may lag behind real upgrades and setup changes.
- Weather forecasts change frequently.
- Early models should be presented as portfolio/demo models until validated on historical seasons.
